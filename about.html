About



Krish Parikh is a student at Queen Mary University of London. He is currently in his first year for a 4-year undergraduate degree in computer science. He was born and raised in Antwerpen, Belgium, and is of Indian descent.

He is a self-motivated and creative student who has had a lifelong interest in technology, electronics, and the digital age. Krish is focused on achieving his goal of studying computer science and making a difference in the world. He has worked on projects and used a variety of online resources that can help him improve in this field. His long-term goal is to expand his knowledge and gain expertise in computer science, as well as to understand the evolution of technology and to be a part of it.

"The developing nature of technology fascinates me", he says, "and I am keen on doing my part in advancing this. I learned about networks, webpages, and programming through online and personal mentors. Soon I realized that computer science being sole "programming and hacking" was a common misconception, while it is more about logical thinking, problem-solving, and storing information."
